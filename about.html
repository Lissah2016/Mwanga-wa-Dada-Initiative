About Us

Who We Are

Mwanga wa Dada Initiative is a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering girls in rural communities through mentorship, dignity, and digital access. We focus on addressing the real and everyday challenges that prevent girls from reaching their full potential — from lack of guidance and life skills to limited access to menstrual health support and technology.

Our approach is simple but impactful: equip girls with the confidence, knowledge, and tools they need to stay in school, make informed decisions, and build a better future for themselves. By working closely with communities, we aim to create sustainable change that extends beyond the individual girl to her family and community.

At Mwanga wa Dada, we believe that when you empower a girl, you empower a generation.

Our Story

Mwanga wa Dada Initiative was founded with a vision to address the challenges faced by girls in underserved communities. Many girls grow up without mentorship, miss school due to lack of menstrual health support, and have limited exposure to opportunities in the digital world.

We believe that with the right support, every girl can rise above these challenges and create a brighter future for herself and her community.

Our Mission

To empower girls with the knowledge, support, and resources they need to stay in school, build confidence, and unlock their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where every girl, regardless of her background, has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed.

What We Do

  • Mentorship and life skills programs
  • Menstrual health and dignity support
  • Digital literacy and exposure
